Latitude vs. Humidity

It can be seen in the chart above that there is no strong correlation between Latitude and Humidity. However, there is an interest observation that can be made for locations further up in the northern hemisphere. The data points appears to be a greater number of data points that are clustered together. Therefore, areas higher up in the northern hemishpere primarily experience humidity greater than 50%.
